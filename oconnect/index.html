ONE STEP, a rotating, easy-to-plug-in electrical outlet

A student venture based at UNIST (Jun 2020 – Feb 2023), founded to address usability challenges in everyday life — designed to enable more intuitive and convenient plug insertion.

Led by CEO Hyeong-Jun Bang  ·  Tech Lead Jinseo Park  ·  Designer Kyuhwan Shim
